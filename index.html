Biography

  • He joined Alibaba in 2018. Now, he is a senior algorithm engineer in Alibaba DAMO Academy. He has published more than 20 papers in international journals and top conferences such as CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ICLR, AAAI and ACM MM. His current researches are mainly on self-supervised/semi-supervised/unsupervised learning and domain adaptation.

  • Weihua Chen obtained his Ph.D. degree in National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), as a member of Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing (CRIPAC). His supervisor is Prof. Kaiqi Huang. And he received the Bachelor and Master degree under Prof. Zhenjiang Miao's advice in Schoole of Computer and Information Technology from Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU).
